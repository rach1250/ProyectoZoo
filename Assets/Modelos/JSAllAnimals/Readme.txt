Thank you for downloading Complete J. Suko Animals Pack.

This package contains 3d model and animations of animals.
This package contains
11 invertebrate(Ammonite, Anomalocaris,Ceratocanthus, Shrimp, Snowcrab, Trilobite, Scorpion, Tarantula, Japanese gold beetle, Ant, Hornet)
2 amphibians(FrogAndSkeleton, PoisonDartFrog)
18 birds(Archaeopteryx, BaldEagle, BarnOwl, BowerBird, Cassowary, HarpyEagle, HummingBird, Penguin, Pigeon, RedCrownedCrane, Swan, Ostrich, Secretarybird, Moa, European robin, Crow, Chicken, Seagull)
16 fishes(Dunkleosteus, Sardine, Seahorse, Angelfish, Arowana, Pirarucu, Shark, Black rock fish, Japanese horse mackerel, Mahi mahi, Tuna, Ayu, Salmon, Betta, Koi, Goldfish)
33 mammals(Deer, Capybara, Maned wolf, Orca, Bear(Poler bear and Panda Textures were also included.), Gorilla, Jerboa, Earless seal, Rhinoceros, Giraffe, Lion(Tiger texture is also included.), Wildebeest, Elephant, Impala, Zebra, Paraceratherium, Hippopotamus, Cattle, Camel, Tapir, Beaver, Horse, Giant anteater, Cat, Greyhound, Rabbit, Squirrel, Pig, Armadillo, Wolf, Dolphin, Goat, Cheetah).
18 reptiles(6 dinosaurus(Stegosaurus, Spinosaurus, AllosaurusAndSkeleton, Gallimimus, Triceratops, Diplodocus), Mosasaurus, 4 lizards(Gecko, Iguana, Komododragon, Leaf-tailed Gecko),6 turtles
(AlligatorSnappingTurtle, BoxTurtle, RedfootedTurtoise, SeaTurtle, AfricanSpurredTortoise, Giant tortoise), Crocodile).
